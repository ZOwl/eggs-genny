Usage

This section just overviews the choice of Gulp as a task runner, in addition to general how-tos and where-are-they's of using eggs-genny to build your projects.

A quick word on gulp: By default, eggs-genny uses Gulp as a task-runner (porting it to Grunt soon, as well as a pure npm-based solution!). Gulp was chosen because of it's simplicity as well as it's 'one-plugin/one-thing' mentality. Once you use Gulp and wrangle the concept of task dependency (i.e: Task B is dependent on Task A being done before it can start it's thing) it's super easy to get it to do whatever you want, synchronously or async. If you are a Grunt fan, help out the project by developing a Grunt build option (as I know more about Gulp than I do Grunt).
